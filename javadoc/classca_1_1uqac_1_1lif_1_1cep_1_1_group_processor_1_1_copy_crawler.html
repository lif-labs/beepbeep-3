Detailed Description

Crawler that creates copies (clones) of whatever it encounters on its way and re-pipes processors as in the original group.

Caveat emptor: the starting point of the crawl cannot be a processor with output arity of 0. Otherwise, none of the processors in the new group will be piped together.
